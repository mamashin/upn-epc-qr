This service allows you to scan UPN QR codes (used in Slovenia) and obtain payment details in EPC QR format compatible with Revolut, Bunq, N26 and other EU banks.

Scan - scan QR with a camera

Enter - manually enter payment details

Edit - edit the scanned code

After scanning the QR code, you can choose to either copy the link to share it or save it for later use.
